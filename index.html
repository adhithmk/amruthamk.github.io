Dr. Amrutha M K

Assistant Professor of English | Digital Humanities Researcher | Interdisciplinary Scholar

Welcome to my academic portfolio. I am passionate about exploring the intersections of literature, culture, and technology through the lens of digital humanities.

My research focuses on developing innovative methodologies that bridge traditional humanities scholarship with digital tools and approaches, particularly in the context of South Asian literary and performance traditions.

Through my work, I aim to demonstrate how digital technologies can enhance our understanding of cultural heritage and create new possibilities for scholarly engagement with traditional texts and performances.
